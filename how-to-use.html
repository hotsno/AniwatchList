AniwatchList

How to use

This should be pretty self explanatory, but enter your AniList username in the search bar, and press the "Submit" button. All of the shows your account has marked as "Watching" should show up. Do note that private accounts and items marked as private on AniList will not show up.

I recommend using a great extension called MAL-Sync to automatically update your AniList to the proper number of episodes watched once you complete an episode on Aniwatch (despite the name it syncs with AniList as well).
